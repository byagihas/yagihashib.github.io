Planet Labs: Over the Surface

Who are Planet Labs and What do they do?

Planet Labs Website

In essence, Planet Labs is a up and coming satellite imagery and software company. By deploying satellites called "Doves", imaging satellites that pictures of the earth daily, the company hopes to provide not only commercial but humanitarian and environmental value with this global imaging network.

Planet labs plans to provide four different sectors with daily global imagery:

  • Agriculture
  • Natural Resources
  • Government
  • Geospatial

Hardware Infrastructure

The first launch of "Doves" saw a flock of 28 satellites launched into Earth's low orbit.

Software Infrastructure

PL is currently developing the Planet Labs API for customers to quickly grab imagery and data from the flocks.
